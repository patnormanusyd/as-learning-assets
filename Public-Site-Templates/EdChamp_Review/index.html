What is a literature review?

Literature refers to a collection of published information/materials on a particular area of reserach or topic, such as books and journal articles of academic value. However, your literature review does not need to be inclusive of every article and bookn that has been written on your topic because that will be too broad. Rather, it should include the key sources related to the main debates, trends and gaps in your research area.
