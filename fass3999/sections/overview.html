A framework for your task

Schneider, J. (2015). Based on the Double Diamond: Strategy and Execution of the Right Solution https://www.thoughtworks.com/insights/blog/double-diamond.

The library has adapted this design thinking framework and broken it down further into five distinct parts that will be mentioned throughout this module:

1. Discover

Unpack your project

identify what you know

identify what you need to know.

2. Interpret

Categorise into information types

Identify the right tools to use.

3. Search

Look for information

Evaluate the data

Evaluate the sources

Share/summarise

4. Experiment

Synthesise new ideas

Defining the problem

Finding Knowledge Gaps

Offering Solutions

Putting together a report

5. Evolve

Bring report to group

Articulate your disciplines value

Challenge and build upon ideas

Create a Unified view
